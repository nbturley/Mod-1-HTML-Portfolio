About Me

Originally from Eastern NC, Nathanael grew up in a small family that spent most of their free time together- enjoying movies, playing games, and attending and serving at church. In high school, Nathanael realized his natural talent for running and led the High School Track team to victory in local and regional meets. Following high school, Nathanael attended the Air Force Academy and Central Carolina Community College where he studied Lasers and Photonics. He has applied his optical and electrical knowledge in many different areas including the alignment of camera lenses and the repair and overhaul of aeronautical lighting. Realizing that in 2023, the world has now changed and he's ready to get with the times, Nathanael has enrolled in Coding Temple Bootcamp to learn Software Development. In his free time, Nathanael still kicks butt in 5k races and goes on epic adventures with his wife (who is still behind the times, wondering what on earth a computer thing is anyway).
